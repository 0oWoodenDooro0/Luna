# Product Guidelines: Luna

## Branding & Personality
Luna embodies an **Atmospheric/Thematic** presence. Every interaction should feel like part of a larger RPG world, using flavorful and evocative language that enhances the player's immersion. While maintaining utility, the bot's communication should feel like a storyteller's narrative, making the hunt management process more engaging.

## UX Principles
- **Clear Feedback Loops:** Every action taken by a player must be met with immediate and unambiguous feedback. Whether a hunt starts, an item is found, or a level is gained, the player should never be in doubt about the outcome of their action.
- **Minimal Channel Clutter:** To ensure a clean and organized Discord environment, Luna will prioritize consolidating information into as few messages as possible. This involves using embeds and editing existing messages where appropriate to avoid overwhelming channels with bot output.
- **Response Efficiency:** Speed is a core tenet of the Luna experience. The bot's systems are designed for low latency, ensuring that players receive prompt responses to their commands, keeping the gameplay flow fast and responsive.

## Content & Prose Style
- **Second Person (Direct):** Luna addresses the player directly using "you" (e.g., "You have successfully started a hunt!"). This approach fosters a direct connection between the player and the game world, making every achievement feel personal.
- **Thematic Vocabulary:** Use terminology consistent with fantasy RPG settings (e.g., "stash," "loot," "experience," "quest") to maintain the thematic consistency of the bot's branding.
- **Concise Narrative:** While being atmospheric, the bot's prose should remain concise. Every word should contribute to either the theme or the utility, avoiding unnecessary verbosity that could slow down the user's interaction.
