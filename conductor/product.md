# Initial Concept
A Discord bot named Luna that manages hunts and player progression.

# Product Guide: Luna

## Product Description
Luna is a Discord bot designed for **RPG Enthusiasts** and **Idle Game Fans** that streamlines the management and tracking of hunts and progression. It provides an efficient and direct way for players to engage with hunt-based mechanics, allowing them to focus on the thrill of the hunt while the bot handles the underlying data and progression systems.

## Vision & Purpose
Luna's core purpose is to simplify **Hunt Management** for players, providing a robust platform for tracking progress and rewards. It aims to bridge the gap between active gameplay and idle progression by offering a seamless way to initialize hunts and collect loot, all within the Discord environment.

## Target Audience
- **RPG Enthusiasts:** Players who enjoy competitive or collaborative RPG mechanics and want a reliable tool for tracking their progress.
- **Idle Game Fans:** Gamers looking for passive progression and collection features that allow them to grow their character over time with minimal active input.

## Core Features
- **Hunt Management:** The primary system for initiating and tracking hunts, providing real-time feedback on progress and results.
- **Leveling & Progression:** A comprehensive system where players earn experience points and level up through successful hunts, unlocking new challenges and rewards.
- **Inventory & Loot System:** A robust system for collecting items, managing a personal stash, and tracking valuable loot obtained during hunts.

## Tone & User Interaction
Luna interacts with users as an **Efficient Utility**. Its communication is functional, direct, and focused on providing the necessary information and feedback to players as quickly as possible. The bot avoids unnecessary fluff, ensuring that the user experience is smooth and focused on the core gameplay loops.
